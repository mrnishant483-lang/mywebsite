How to Protect Your Online Accounts from Hackers

Published: 30 June 2026

Cyber security is becoming more important every year. Students and internet users should know how to protect their online accounts from hackers and scams.

1. Use Strong Passwords

Create passwords with a mix of uppercase letters, lowercase letters, numbers, and special characters. Avoid using your name or date of birth.

2. Enable Two-Factor Authentication

Two-factor authentication (2FA) adds an extra layer of security by requiring a verification code during login.

3. Never Share OTPs

Banks and trusted companies never ask for your OTP or password. Never share these details with anyone.

4. Beware of Fake Websites

Always check the website address before entering personal information. Fake websites often look similar to real ones.

5. Keep Software Updated

Update your browser, apps, and operating system regularly to stay protected from the latest security threats.

Conclusion

Following simple cyber security habits can keep your online accounts safe and protect your personal information from hackers.
